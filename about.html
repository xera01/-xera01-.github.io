We Are Experienced in Power Generation and Supply

DIVINE POWER PRODUCTS is a Power generation company established in 2004 with the goal to provide a pragmatic approach to solving the incessant power problems the country Nigeria is facing.

Divine Power since inception is focused on Providing power to Government Organizations, Corporate organizations, Commercial centers like markets and shopping malls using stand-alone energy systems. These systems range from Micro turbine, mini hydro and solar solutions. The focus on the stand alone applications is to cut off completely the existing transmission and distribution infrastructural deficiencies.

Solutions canvassed by us include, Waste to power, Well to power, micro turbines and solar Power for a wide range of clients spanning Construction Companies, Educational Institutions, Financial institutions, Religious Organizations, Manufacturing Concerns, Real Estate Businesses and a host of others.

In 2007 Divine Power Products was awarded Best Generator Sales and Maintenance Company by the prestigous Institute of Direct Marketing of Nigeria.

In 2009 Divine Power Products was awarded Best West Africa's Electricity Generation and Consultancy Firm of the year by the prestigous Institute of Direct Marketing of Nigeria.

Meet Our Team

Ayo Babajide (CEO)

A 1984 graduate of university of Ife, Mr Babajide joined as a Pioneer staff of Lordmart Nigeria Ltd, a power generation company. His marketing abilities shot the company up to the leading company in power generation in Nigeria between 1989 and 2004. He thereafter resigned as the Head of Sales and Marketing to set up Divine Power Products a wholly indigenous power Generation Company.

Mr. Babajide’s passion is in finding solutions to the unending Power problems in Nigeria and has been involved in different fora with people of like mind to proffer solutions to these problems especially in using the micro Turbine Technology to generate Power. An active participant in Nigerian’s Power Solution Conferences, Mr. Babajide is a regular commentator on Power issues and an acclaimed authority in Nigeria on Power.

He also holds an MBA and a post Graduate Diploma in Theology and Leadership Training. He  sits on a number of Boards including but not limited WTF Construction Ltd, Gas Turbines International Nig. Divine Power Products Cote d’ Ivoire, WTF Design and Build Cote d’Ivoire to mention a few.

Pela Miranghe (Director)

Mr. Miranghe Pela is a 1997 graduate of Civil Engineering from the University of Benin. Post graduation, He has worked with quite a number of top property development companies among who was CITIPROPS LTD. Mr. Pela moved from Citiprops to the Energy giants Trans-Africa Gas & Electric Corporation Plc (TAG & E) where he was part of their pioneering team of their Nigerian operations. He rose through the ranks to become their Managing Director / Chief Operating Officer, a position he relinquished in 2009. Miranghe joined Divine Power Products in January, 2010 as Director

Folorunsho Kolade (Head, Technical)

Engr. Kolade is a 1982 graduate of Electrical Engineering from the University of Ife, now Obafemi Awolowo University. From Post graduation till now he had worked in NEPA, Affam Power Station as maintenance Engineer, Negris Nigeria Limited, also as a Maintenance Engineer from where ha moved to TSG Nigeria – where he rose to the post of Chief Engineer, the first black man to be so appointed in the history of the organization. Mr. Kolade had the benefit of attending many Power and Maintenance related courses both in Nigeria and abroad. He is married with children.
